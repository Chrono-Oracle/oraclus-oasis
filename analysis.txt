Key Terms: Primary Key (PK), Foreign Key (FK)


ENTITIES AND ATTRIBUTES
1) USER: id(PK), name, email, password, role(reader / admin / author), created_at
2) ARTICLE: id(PK), author_id(FK), title, slug, sub_content, content, status(published / draft), created_at, updated_at
3) COMMENT: id(PK), article_id(FK), reader_id(FK), content, created_at
